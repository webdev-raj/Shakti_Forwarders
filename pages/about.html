WHO WE ARE

We take immense pleasure in introducing to you our company Shakti Forwarders. We are Custom clearing agents based in Mumbai, Gujarat and Delhi.

Our services are available at MUMBAI, JNPT, SURAT, AHEMDABAD, KANDLA, MUNDRA and DELHI. We have been efficiently serving our existing clients for their import and export shipments.

We do arrange for a total logistics from the pick up of the cargo at the loading port to the delivery at the final destination. We can assist you with various services like drawback/refund claims license work of DGFT and other custom n license related work.
